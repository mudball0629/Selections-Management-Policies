Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

Effective date: March 27, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how Forner - LaVoy Development LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects and uses information in connection with our project coordination SMS communications. Information We Collect We collect your name and mobile phone number as provided by your construction coordinator as part of an active construction project. How We Use Your Information Your phone number is used solely to send you SMS messages related to your construction project — including selections updates, action requests, appointment confirmations, and coordinator follow-ups. We do not use your information for marketing purposes. Information Sharing We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to deliver messages (e.g., our SMS service provider, Twilio). Opt-Out You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
